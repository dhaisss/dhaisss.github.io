      • WELCOME TO LEGAL ENGLISH COURSE Forum

        Hello my dear students, welcome to Legal English (LE) course of this semester. Nice to meet you all. Hopefully, you are pleased to attend this course. As well as vocabulary and grammar, this course provides many materials for you to develop your LE such as reading, listening, writing, and speaking.

        Good luck and enjoy your class.

    • Topic 1: Basic Legal Terminology

      Topic 1: Basic Legal Terminology

      This course is designed to help you develop your legal English vocabulary. After learning, you are hopefully able to master this basic legal terminology.

      Before working on this course, think of the following question.

      - Do you think that word (vocabulary) is important? If yes, why?

      To have more understanding of the importance of vocabulary, you can access it from the following files/links.

      • The Importance of Vocabulary File

    • Topic 3: People in The Legal System

      Topic 3: People in The Legal System

      This course is designed to help you develop your listening and reading skills. As well as these skills it also enhance your Legal English vocabulary development. To meet these aims, it provides some exercises that you can access from the following files. To complete the exercises, first download the pdf file and second the audio file for exercise one. Read carefully  the instructions on each exercise.

      Before you start your completing the exercises, it is better for your to do brainstorming by answering the questions below.

      -  What are some different jobs in the legal profession?

      -  Which legal job do your prefer, and why?
